The Machinery

7 Specialists.
One Unified Engine.

Not freelancers. Not a one-person show wearing seven hats. A fully integrated team building your system.

01

Funnel Strategist

The architect. Designs the entire system—offer, messaging, journey, and KPIs. Every other specialist works directly from their blueprint.

02

Direct Response Copywriter

The psychologist. Does not write "creative" fluff. Writes cold emails, ad copy, and sales pages specifically engineered to provoke action and pull credit cards.

03

UI/UX Conversion Designer

The builder. Maps the visual flow of the landing pages and forms, prioritizing the path of least resistance for a user to convert.

04

Media Buyer

The traffic controller. Manages ad spend relentlessly across Meta, TikTok, and Google. Their only job is dropping the Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) while maintaining lead quality.

05

Video Editor / Motion Graphics

The hook master. Cuts short-form VSLs and ad creatives meant to stop the scroll. Relies heavily on visual pacing and dynamic captions.

06

Automation Engineer

The plumber. Wires Zapier, Make, HighLevel, and custom webhooks so your leads hit your phone and email sequences the exact second they opt-in.

07

Client Success Manager

The liaison. Translates the data into terms you understand. Ensures the system is delivering precisely what was promised in the blueprint.
