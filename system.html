The System

The BrandKings
5-Phase System

Every phase is built in full before you sign anything. All 5 activate simultaneously on Day 1.

01
Phase One

Traffic

We design and deploy multi-channel traffic campaigns targeted at your specific niche audience. Every campaign is built around cost-per-qualified-lead, not vanity metrics. This includes paid traffic across Meta, TikTok, and Google, as well as organic content distribution architected for discovery in your niche.

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)
TikTok Ads
Google Ads (Search + Display)
Niche audience targeting
Ad creative production
A/B testing framework
02
Phase Two

Lead Capture

We build all landing pages, opt-in flows, and lead capture infrastructure. Every page is conversion-optimised and tested before launch. When a visitor lands on your system, there's a clear, compelling path to becoming a lead — and it's automated from the moment they opt in.

Landing page builds
Opt-in flow design
Lead magnet creation
CRM integration
Conversion rate optimisation
Thank-you page sequences
03
Phase Three

Conversion

This is where leads become clients. We build all follow-up systems, booking funnels, and conversion infrastructure so that every lead immediately enters an automated process designed to bring them to a sale or a booked call. No manual outreach required — the system does it.

Booking system setup
AI response bot
Email nurture sequences
SMS follow-up flows
WhatsApp automation
Offer & pricing positioning
04
Phase Four

Expansion

Acquiring a new client is the beginning, not the end. The Expansion phase maximises the lifetime value of every client through retargeting sequences, upsell flows, referral triggers, and review generation systems. These run automatically.

Retargeting campaigns
Upsell sequences
Referral trigger system
Review generation flow
Loyalty & re-engagement
Repeat purchase automation
05
Phase Five

Automation

This is the phase that makes it all run without you. AI-powered workflows, CRM automations, and scheduling systems handle every repetitive process in the client acquisition pipeline — 24/7, without human intervention. Your time is freed while your system keeps running.

Full CRM automation
AI chatbot deployment
Calendar & scheduling automation
Lead scoring workflows
Pipeline management
Weekly performance reporting
